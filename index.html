Welcome to Refined Designs – a Canberra-based business specialising in the design and delivery of wedding invitations and other stationery. We understand that organising a wedding can be a stressful time. To try to make the process easier, we bring our designs to you and deliver them to you when they are complete.

While we specialise in the design of wedding invitations we will gladly accommodate all of your invitation needs so please do not hesitate to contact us whatever the occasion.

Please feel free to browse our website and if you would like any further information or to arrange a meeting, please contact us by phone or email.

Invitations

We offer many different types of invitations which are all customisable and can be made to match other stationery. If you have designed your own, we're also happy to help.

FAQs

Do you have a question about the invitation process? Read our FAQs. If you have further questions, email us.

Pricing

The price list is a guide only. It you would like an exact quote, email or call us to arrange a meeting to discuss your requirements.
