about us

Refined Designs is a Canberra-based business specialising in the design and delivery of stylish wedding invitations. We offer both contemporary and classic designs, catering for all tastes and budgets. We understand that organising a wedding can be a very stressful time and we would like to help you. We take the hassle out of your wedding preparations as we can bring our designs to you. We do not offer a do-it-yourself option, but instead deliver a completed wedding package for your special day.

Whilst we specialise in the design of wedding invitations we will gladly accommodate all your invitation needs so please do not hesitate to contact us whatever the occasion.

Please feel free to browse our website and if you would like any further information about the suites we offer or would like an obligation free quote please contact us by phone or email to arrange a meeting time.
