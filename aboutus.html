ABOUT US

I started Refined Designs in 2009. When I made my own wedding invitations back in 2007, I found it to be a very difficult and stressful process! I'm a perfectionist and because it was my own wedding, I wanted everything to be perfect. Now that I run a business making invitations and other stationery, I love it!

I understand that organising a wedding can be a very stressful and busy time so I enjoy being able to help out with wedding stationery. To try to make the process easier, I bring my designs to you and deliver them to you when they are complete.

I will gladly accommodate all of your invitation needs so please do not hesitate to contact me whatever the occasion.

If you would like any further information or to arrange a meeting, please get in touch by phone or email.
