BOMBONIERE

A bomboniere is a small thank you gift given to guests at weddings and other occasions. We usually offer three heart chocolates in the colour of your choice for the inside of the bomboniere but will happily accommodate other requests.

We offer a a variety of boxes and prisms that can also include a placecard and thank you message.

The price for all of the bomboniere includes three chocolates and full assembly.
